Frequently asked

Questions OKC homeowners ask us

Don’t see yours? Call us at (405) 436-4776.

Is Spark Shark Electric licensed?
Yes. We operate under Oklahoma Electrical License #163603, issued by the Oklahoma Construction Industries Board. Every electrician dispatched to your home works under that contractor license.
What does the on-time promise cover?
If our technician doesn’t arrive within your scheduled appointment window, the service-call fee for that visit is waived. The promise applies to all standard residential service appointments scheduled in advance.
Do you really answer the phone 24/7?
Yes — a real person answers the phone every day, day or night. After-hours emergency calls go straight to a licensed Oklahoma electrician who can get to your home the same night when needed.
How is pricing handled?
No-surprises flat-rate. You receive a written quote before any work begins; that quoted price is the final invoice price. There’s no hourly meter and no surprise add-on at the end.
What areas do you serve?
The full Oklahoma City metro: Oklahoma City, Moore, Edmond, Norman, Yukon, Mustang, Midwest City, Choctaw, Noble, Del City, Newcastle, and Blanchard. Outside that footprint? Call us — we still might be able to help.
Are you BBB Accredited?
Yes. Spark Shark Electric has been an accredited business with the Better Business Bureau serving Central Oklahoma since July 14, 2025.
