=== FAQ ===
Q: Can you add an outlet without tearing up my wall?
A: Often, yes. Especially in older homes with accessible attic or crawlspace. We fish the wire and patch only where absolutely necessary.

Q: Why does my GFCI keep tripping?
A: Three causes: a real ground fault on the circuit (something is leaking to ground), a shared neutral on the protected circuit, or a failing GFCI device. We can identify which on the visit.

Q: Do smart switches need a neutral?
A: Most modern smart switches do require a neutral wire at the switch box. Older Moore and OKC homes often don't have one, we can pull a neutral or recommend a no-neutral-required smart switch model.

Q: Are USB outlets a fire hazard?
A: Quality-brand USB outlets installed correctly are safe. The cheap ones from auction sites are not, we install brand-name UL-listed devices.
