=== FAQ ===
Q: Can you add cans without tearing up my ceiling?
A: In most finished ceilings, yes, we use remodel cans that mount through the drywall opening. Sometimes there's a small access cut needed for a junction box.

Q: Why do my LED lights flicker?
A: Usually a dimmer mismatch. Older incandescent dimmers don't drive LEDs well. They need a dimmer rated for LED loads. We match the dimmer to the lamp.

Q: Can you do landscape-grade lighting indoors?
A: Yes. Accent and uplighting indoors uses similar principles. We do those regularly in finished basements and great rooms.

Q: How many cans should I have in a room?
A: Rough rule: one 4-inch LED can per 12 sq ft of ceiling area for general lighting, more or fewer based on ceiling height and the look you want.
