=== FAQ ===
Q: Low-voltage or line-voltage?
A: Path and landscape lighting is almost always low-voltage (12V), safer to work around and easier to extend later. Security lighting and floods are typically line-voltage (120V).

Q: Can you add lighting to existing landscaping?
A: Yes. That's most of what we do. We trench shallow runs, set fixtures, and tie back to a transformer at a GFCI-protected outdoor outlet.

Q: Do outdoor outlets need to be GFCI?
A: Yes. Any outdoor outlet must have GFCI protection per current code.

Q: How long do landscape lights last?
A: Quality LED fixtures should run 10+ years on the LED itself. Wiring and transformers should last the same.
