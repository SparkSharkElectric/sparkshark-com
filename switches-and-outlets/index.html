FAQ

Common questions

Can you add an outlet without tearing up my wall?

Often, yes — especially in older homes with accessible attic or crawlspace. We fish the wire and patch only where absolutely necessary.

Why does my GFCI keep tripping?

Three causes: a real ground fault on the circuit (something is leaking to ground), a shared neutral on the protected circuit, or a failing GFCI device. We can identify which on the visit.

Do smart switches need a neutral?

Most modern smart switches do require a neutral wire at the switch box. Older Moore and OKC homes often don't have one — we can pull a neutral or recommend a no-neutral-required smart switch model.

Are USB outlets a fire hazard?

Quality-brand USB outlets installed correctly are safe. The cheap ones from auction sites are not — we install brand-name UL-listed devices.
